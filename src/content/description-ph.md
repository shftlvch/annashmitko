Hi,
I'm Anna, a documentary photographer and cinematographer with a focus on social issues, LGBTQ+ stories, and cultural documentation. My work spans institutional exhibitions, theatre production photography, film key art, music videos, and photojournalism.

## Theatre

### Gogol Center / Kirill Serebrennikov (2011–2019)

Key photographic collaborator across major stage projects, responsible for production photography, press materials and poster/key-visual shoots. Built a long-term archive that circulated widely in national cultural media and now functions as durable documentation of landmark productions.

Created the main poster image for "A Midsummer Night's Dream" and produced performance photography for press and documentation.
